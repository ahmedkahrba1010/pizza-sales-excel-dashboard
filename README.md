# pizza-sales-excel-dashboard

An interactive Excel sales dashboard analyzing pizza order data. Features dynamic slicers for size, category, and date alongside visual insights into peak order hours, weekly order volumes, and top-selling pizza types to optimize sales performance.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview
<img width="1320" height="529" alt="meddoo" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fa9915b5-3983-43e4-8fcb-edf9c0fa4263" />


This interactive Excel dashboard provides an end-to-end visual analysis of pizza sales performance, order volumes, and consumer behavior trends. Designed with dynamic filtering, it allows stakeholders to quickly isolate key metrics by size, category, day, and date ranges.

### Key Visualizations & Insights

* **Total Sum by Quantity & Pizza Size:** A donut chart showing sales distribution across sizes. Large (**L - 40%**) and Small (**S - 30%**) pizzas drive the majority of the volume.
* **Total Sales by Day:** A horizontal bar chart tracking revenue throughout the week. **Friday** stands out as the peak revenue day at **$1,535**, followed closely by Saturday.
* **Total Sales by Pizza Type:** A pie chart breaking down popularity by category, led by **Veggie (28%)** and **Classic (27%)**.
* **Order by Hours:** A continuous line chart capturing peak operational hours. Demand spikes significantly in the late afternoon and early evening, peaking at **5:00 PM with 72 orders**, before sharply tapering off by 11:00 PM.
* **Total Order by Week:** A vertical bar chart mapping order consistency across the weeks, peaking during **Week 7 with 91 orders**.

### 🎛️ Interactive Control Filters (Slicers)

The dashboard is equipped with interactive slicers to drill down into specific data subsets:
* **Pizza Size:** Filter by L, M, S, or XL.
* **Week Day:** Isolate specific days of the week.
* **Pizza Category:** Filter by Classic, Supreme, Veggie, or Chicken.
* **Order Date Timeline:** A chronological slider to filter performance across specific dates (e.g., September 2015).

## 🚀 Features
